Plannig the Destination

Well planed journey is a important for consider.

Be well prepared, equiped before you go.

Use a appropriate shoes and jacket. It is necessary. Sometimes a torch is very nice thing to have. Especially at night. Also a lighter. In case when you need make a fire.

Always check the weather before you go.

A lot of people were surprised by weather in the nature.

Tell to your relatives where you go.

It is one important and necessary things. You never know what is going to happen there. Might you brake leg or get lost. One never know what else can happen.

In the forest it is very simple. Youcan go where do you want. Keep you eeyes open wide. Then you can be creative. Enjoy the nature and do not make so much noice. Then all animals run away and you just was on a walk. Even walk can help.

Mountain

For mountain walk be always well prepared. The weather can change every hour. Do not get lost therefore rather take a map. Even you got a phone. Your battery run out or you accidentaly breake the phone. Then its hard get back.

Beach

Before you leave house count with the tide. Rather check the tide times on net. Then count the journey to the sea and how long do you going to spent time on the beach.

Desert

The desert is not very well place to stay. Therefore drink a water. Consider how much water left for you and how long it takes you out off the deset where is naother water. Use also a head cover (hat). It keeps you cover before the sun. Better have some cover on body even is too hot. Direct sun shine can be automatically end of you journey.
